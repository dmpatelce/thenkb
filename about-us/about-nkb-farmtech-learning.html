Overview

NKB Farmtech Learning is one of the most admired talent transformation organisations, enabling sustainable business & social impact through technology enabled solutions. An ISO 9001:2015 certified company, its mission is to provide training that impacts business productivity. NKB Farmtech Learning, with over 2700 learning and development specialists and their domain expertise in 21+ industry verticals, has trained more than 1.8 million people globally.

NKB Farmtech Learning focuses on five key areas:

  • Corporate Training
  • Corporate Social Responsibility Partnerships
  • Learning Experience Platform (LXP/LMS)
  • Vocational Education and Training
  • Skills for Schools and Colleges

With the legacy of Bharti group and rich experience of over 14 years in learning & development space, NKB Farmtech Learning has provided customized end-to-end training solutions that impact business to more than 400 corporates globally. Using its Technology enabled Blended Learning (TeBL®) methodology and use of its learning experience platform (LXP) – NKB Farmtech Learning is providing 360 degree training solutions from entry level to middle management.

NKB Farmtech Learning has successfully partnered with Central and State Ministries, Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) as well as with multiple Public Sector Enterprises. In India, NKB Farmtech Learning has set up NKB Farmtech WorkSkills India in partnership with NSDC with an objective to enhance skills of millions of youth across the country and empower them with livelihood linked skilling programs. Through its skilling centres across India, it imparts employability linked skills training in sectors like Retail, Automobile, Hospitality, Health Care, Building and Construction, Telecom and Beauty & Wellness, to name a few.

NKB Farmtech Learning is playing a pivotal role in the integration of vocational and school education by implementing National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) in schools and providing vocational education from class 9 onwards. It has partnered with various State governments to implement the ‘Vocationalisation of Higher Secondary Education (VHSE)’ in more than 1950 schools, impacting more than 2,00,000 students across 20 states and union territories in India

The CSR arm of NKB Farmtech Learning- NKB Farmtech Foundation, implements CSR projects for various corporates by providing livelihood skilling to underprivileged youth including people with disabilities. It is a society registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and holds an 80G and FCRA certificate. The society conducts programs impacting national issues in the field of skill development, women welfare and empowerment, vocational training and health awareness.

NKB Farmtech has executed projects in USA, UAE, Myanmar, Nepal, Bangladesh and Sri Lanka, to name a few and is currently working aggressively in African continent towards providing end-to- end training & skilling solutions for various corporates across various sectors. From enhancing skills of banking professionals, to making the youth self-reliant as well as employable through digital literacy, NKB Farmtech Learning is moving ahead in the field of learning and development in the African region.

NKB Farmtech has been widely recognized across various respectable forums- it has received Brandon Hall Award for ‘Best Use of Blended Learning’ 2020 and has been featured as the ‘Top 15 Emerging Leaders in Training Outsourcing’ worldwide. It has also been Ranked #11 amongst India's Great Place to Work® 2019.

Our Group

Bharti Group was founded in 1976, by Sunil Bharti Mittal, Bharti has come a long way from being a manufacturer of bicycle parts to one of the largest and most respected business conglomerates in India. Bharti forayed into Telecom services business by launching cellular services in Delhi (India) in 1995. Over the past few years, the group has diversified into emerging business areas in the fast expanding Indian economy. With its entrepreneurial spirit and passion to undertake business projects that are transformational in nature, Bharti has created world-class businesses in telecom, financial services, retail, foods and Training & Skill Development.
