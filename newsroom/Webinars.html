Webinars

Welcome to Webinar Series - hosted by Centum Learning

As a part of #CentumKnowledgeSeries, we bring together industry practitioners to share their views and experiences, through the webinar, on topics which matter in the learning & development domain. The webinar series aims to provide insightful discussions by industry stalwarts on issues that directly impact key industry domains. t is an excellent platform for HR Professionals, L&D teams, Trainers & Business Heads to interact with industry experts who are passionate about what they do and know more about the best practices. Keep watching this space for all the upcoming & previous webinars.
