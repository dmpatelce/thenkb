“What is the recipe for successful achievement? To my mind there are just four essential ingredients: Choose a career you love, give it the best there is in you, seize your opportunities, and be a member of the team.” - Benjamin F. Fairless

Centum Learning is an organization with an exponential growth rate in an industry where demand far outstrips supply. It is also an organization where each and every employee has the opportunity to make a significant contribution and be recognized, and not merely be a ‘resource’. Centum Learning offers the best of both worlds: the advantages of being part of a relatively young, agile and rapidly growing organization, as well as the benefits of being part of one of the country’s largest conglomerates.

Centum Learning is among the Top 10 ‘mid-size’ organization by Great Place to Work®. Each year, Great Place To Work® Institute recognizes the 100 best workplaces as ‘India’s Best Companies To Work For- 2017’ from organizations having an employee strength of more than 500 and represent 20 industry sectors. Centum Learning’s meteoric rise to become one of the top players in the Training and Skill Development space in the world is entirely due to its people. People are its main drivers. Thus, a lot of care is taken to choose the right people for the right job. A few factors though, serve as a common denominator:

  • A passion towards transforming individuals, organizations and society as a whole - Be the change!
  • The ability to work in a multi-cultural environment - We have a diverse environment where all of us come together to achieve one goal.
  • The ability to master multiple domains - From retail to automobile, from Delhi to Africa we are spread across, the key is to manage all this without losing focus.
  • The attitude of a team player - The power of a team and the sheer force of being a team player is unimaginable. It’s a key to success.
  • The ability to communicate with lucidity - what we say, how we say, what we do and what we present…all great ideas have 80% of chance of being a success if we communicate crystal clear!
  • The ability to quickly grasp concepts and convert them into solutions - To think on your toes and be a solution provider is how we have gained our reputation of being a problem fixer.

1. Application & screening Job opening with centum learning in one of the following ways:

  • Fill the standard application form. You will be required to fill in a few basic details and upload your resume. We will review your application and get back to you as and when we are hiring for job profiles suitable to your skills and experience.
  • You can browse through the current openings and send us a mail at HR.Communication@centumlearning.com with the subject line mentioning the exact position as mentioned on the page.

2. Assessment

Upon receiving a call from us you will have to take 4 written test. These tests assess your proficiency and help us in evaluating you in the areas of:

  • Proficiency in communicating in English language
  • General Aptitude
  • Managerial Skills
  • Psychometric Testing

These tests are conducted at one of our office locations.

3. Interview

Depending on the position applied for and on the basis of your performance in the assessment stage, you will go through 2 to 5 rounds of personal interviews.

4. Verification

You will be required to submit the following:

  • Last drawn salary slips for 3 consecutive months (If applicable)
  • Copy of the Appointment Letter from your current organization (If applicable)

5. Joining

Upon acceptance of the offer, you will have to submit copies of numerous documents like Educational Certificates, Experience Certificates, Address Proof, PAN Card etc.

Post this, you will be introduced to your new team and will be taken through an induction and orientation process with regards to your department, job deliverables and other relevant details.

You will have then, finally become a part of a fast expanding Multi - National Organization which is a leader in the areas of Skilling and Training in 15 countries across two Continents!
